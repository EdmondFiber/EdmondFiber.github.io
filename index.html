The Current Scenario

Currently, Internet Service Providers are able to do some un-fun things.

Charging

Because ISPs have a virtual monopoly, they can charge more for worse service.

No Privacy

ISPs can monitor which websites you visit — and sell that info to advertisers.

Slow Speeds

ISPs have slower speeds than towns with municipal fiber.

Advantages of Municipal Fiber

A municipal fiber network — internet as a utility, administered by the City — has multiple advantages.

Reasonable Pricing

Because it is a service run by the government, the Internet only needs to cost enough to break even on costs.

Privacy Secured

The government will not collect data to sell to advertisers.

Fiber Speeds

Municipal fiber could offer speeds of up to 1 Gbps, much faster than current Internet.

How do we get Municipal Fiber?

Tell the government you want it.

ISPs will put up a fight. Do not let them dissuade you: Municipal Fiber is the best option for fast, cheap internet.
